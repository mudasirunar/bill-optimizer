The Problem

Pakistani electricity bills are a black box — until now.

Slab jumps, protected-status thresholds, fuel adjustments and time-of-use surcharges make it almost impossible to know what you'll owe until the bill arrives.

Sudden Slab Jumps

Cross a 100 or 200-unit threshold and your entire bill is charged at a much higher per-unit rate — often without warning.

Protected Status Loss

Exceed the 6-month rolling average and you permanently lose subsidised Protected or Lifeline pricing.

Peak-Hour Surcharges

Time-of-Use consumers pay up to 25% more for electricity used between 6 PM and 10 PM — easy to miss, costly to ignore.

Questions

Frequently asked questions.

Do I need a smart meter to use this?
No. The engine works entirely from your appliance inventory, property details, and past bills — no hardware or smart-meter integration required.
How accurate are the predictions?
The Random Forest model achieves an R² of roughly 0.84 on held-out PRECON households, and accuracy improves further as you add your own bill history for calibration.
Is my household data private?
Your profile is stored per-account in Cloud Firestore behind Firebase Authentication, and is only used to power your own predictions and the AI assistant.
Which DISCOs and tariff categories are supported?
All major Pakistani DISCOs, plus Lifeline, Protected, Non-Protected, and Time-of-Use categories under NEPRA's July 2026 tariff rules.
