NEPRA Tariff Rates

Official 2026 tariff structures, consumer slabs, and rebased rates for Pakistan.

Official Advisory

Residential tariffs are rebased for the 2026 Calendar Year. These rates are subject to Monthly Fuel Price Adjustments (FCA) and Quarterly Adjustments (QTA).

Consumer Categories

Lifeline

Lifeline Consumer

Single-phase connection with consumption under 100 units consistently for the past 12 months. Lowest protected rate.

Protected

Protected Consumer

Consistently below 200 units for the last 6 consecutive months. Eligible for subsidized slab pricing.

Unprotected

Unprotected / Standard

Crossed the 200-unit threshold even once in the last 6 months. Full commercial tariff rates apply.
